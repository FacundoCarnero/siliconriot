// The Story

Stadium Grandeur

Silicon Riot is the high-octane fusion of 80s arena rock and cyberpunk aesthetics. Born in the heart of a digital metropolis, their debut A Night in Neo-City serves as the ultimate soundtrack for a world of neon lights and high-speed chrome, while their sophomore album Neon Ghosts & Iron Dreams plunges deeper into the gritty machinery of tomorrow.

Fusing glam metal energy with synthwave grit, Silicon Riot brings the spirit of the legends into a rain-soaked, high-tech future.
